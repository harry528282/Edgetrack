Connect Your Notion

EdgeTrack reads your trade journal directly from Notion. Follow the steps below to connect your database in about 2 minutes.

1
Create a Notion integration
Go to notion.so/my-integrations and click New integration. Give it any name (e.g. EdgeTrack), select your workspace, and click Save.
2
Copy your integration token
On the integration page, click Show next to the Internal Integration Secret. Copy that value — it starts with ntn_.
3
Connect your database
Open your trade journal database in Notion. Click the ··· menu in the top right, go to Connections, and add the integration you just created.
4
Copy your database URL
Open your trade journal database in Notion and copy the full URL from your browser address bar. Paste it into the field below — we'll extract the ID automatically.
