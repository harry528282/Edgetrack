EdgeVantage

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

EdgeVantage is a trading analytics dashboard that reads a trade journal you keep in Notion and renders performance metrics. This policy describes exactly what data we handle and why. It is deliberately short because EdgeVantage is deliberately small — there is no marketing pixel, no analytics tracker, no ad network, and nothing sold to anyone.

What we collect

When you connect your Notion workspace to EdgeVantage, we store:

  • A Notion access token granted by you through Notion's OAuth flow (or pasted by you as an internal integration token). This token is scoped only to the pages and databases you explicitly shared with EdgeVantage.
  • Your Notion workspace ID and name, as returned by Notion when the connection is made.
  • The database ID of the trade journal you choose to analyse, and its name.
  • The column mapping you configure (which Notion properties map to "date", "R value", etc.).
  • Your UI preferences (theme, panel layouts, tile positions) if you choose to sync them to your account rather than keep them in your browser.

We do not store a copy of your trade journal rows. Every time you load the dashboard, the rows are fetched from Notion on demand, rendered in your browser, and discarded when you close the tab.

Where it is stored

All account data is stored in Cloudflare Workers KV, a managed key-value store operated by Cloudflare. The Notion access token is encrypted at rest using AES-GCM with a key held server-side; the database ID, mapping, and preferences are stored as plain JSON in the same record.

Session cookies and session tokens are signed with HMAC-SHA-256 so the server can verify them without a database lookup.

Who it is shared with

No one. EdgeVantage does not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. The only external service your data passes through is Notion's API — which is unavoidable, because that is where your trade journal lives and where the access token is issued.

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising, tracking pixels, or session replay tools.

How long it is kept

Your user record is kept as long as you use EdgeVantage. If you do not log in for 90 days, the record expires automatically from Cloudflare KV. You can also delete your account at any time from the dashboard's Settings menu — this immediately erases your encrypted token, mapping, and preferences from our storage.

Deleting your EdgeVantage account does not delete your Notion database. Your trade journal is your own — EdgeVantage only reads from it.

Your rights

You can at any time:

  • Disconnect — log out of EdgeVantage. Your account record stays but the session ends.
  • Delete your account — from the dashboard's Settings menu. This immediately purges your record.
  • Revoke access from Notion's side — go to your Notion integrations page and remove EdgeVantage. The access token becomes immediately invalid; the next time EdgeVantage tries to use it, it will fail.
  • Request a copy of your data — raise an issue at the GitHub repository linked below. Your EdgeVantage record is small (a few kilobytes of JSON) and we will send it to you.

Under UK GDPR you also have rights of rectification, restriction, and complaint to the Information Commissioner's Office. EdgeVantage is a personal project, not a company, but we will honour these rights in good faith.

Cookies

EdgeVantage sets a single cookie, et_session, which contains your signed session token. It is used only to keep you logged in. It is not used for tracking, analytics, or advertising. The same token is also stored in your browser's localStorage as a fallback when cookies are unavailable.

Security

Notion access tokens are encrypted before being written to storage. Session identifiers are signed with HMAC so they cannot be forged. All traffic between your browser and EdgeVantage runs over HTTPS. However, no online service is perfectly secure, and EdgeVantage is provided without warranty — see the terms of use.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, the "last updated" date above will change and — if the change reduces your rights or expands what we collect — we will show a notice in EdgeVantage before it takes effect.

Contact

EdgeVantage is maintained by an individual developer as an open-source project. For privacy questions, data requests, or account deletion assistance, raise an issue at github.com/harry528282/EdgeVantage/issues. If the matter is sensitive (e.g. a security report or a data deletion request containing account identifiers), open a private advisory via the repository's Security tab instead of a public issue.
